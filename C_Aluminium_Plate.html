Aluminium Plate

6061 Aluminum Sheet & Plate offers a combination of higher strength, good corrosion resistance and machinability making the 6061 grade the most widely used aluminum sheet and plate grade available for all types of fabrication projects. The general purpose 6061 Aluminum Sheet & Plate is heat treatable, resists cracking due to stress, and is easy to weld and machine, but limited on formability. 6061 Aluminum Sheet & Plate are ideal for structural framing, base plates, gussets, aircraft, marine and automotive parts, etc.
