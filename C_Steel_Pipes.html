Steel Pipes

ASTM A 53:

Specification

Steel Pipe, Black / Hot-Dipped, Zinc-Coated, Welded / Seamless ASTM A53 (ASME SA53) carbon steel pipe is a specification that covers seamless and welded black and hot-dipped galvanized steel pipe in NPS 1/8″ to NPS 26. A 53 is intended for pressure and mechanical applications and is also acceptable for ordinary uses in steam, water, gas, and air lines.

A53 pipe comes in three types (F, E, S) and two grades (A, B). A53 Type F is manufactured with a furnace butt weld or may have a continuous weld (Grade A only)
A53 Type E has an electric resistance weld (Grades A and B)
A53 Type S is a seamless pipe and found in Grades A and B)
A53 Grade B Seamless is our most polar product under this specification and A53 pipe is commonly dual certified to A106 B Seamless pipe.

Sizes: NPS 1/2” to NPS 26” O.D. Wall thickness: Schedules 10 through 160, STD
Application: HVAC / Plumbing & Firefighting / Transportation Oil & Gas / High Pressure Applications / Chemical Processing

ASTM A 106

ASTM A106 seamless pressure pipe is commonly used in the construction of oil and gas refineries, power plants, petrochemical plants, boilers, and ships where the piping must transport fluids and gases that exhibit higher temperatures and pressure levels.

ASTM A106/ASME SA106 is the standard specification for seamless carbon steel pipe applied for high temperature services. It includes three grades A, B and C, and common use grade is A106 Grade B. It used in different industries not only for pipeline systems like oil and gas, water, mineral slurry transmission, but also for boiler, construction, structural purposes.
ASTM A106 Grade B pipe is equivalent to ASTM A53 Grade B and API 5L B on chemical position and mechanical properties, in general use carbon steel and yield strength minimum 240 MPa, tensile strength 415 Mpa.

BS1387

Description

BS 1387 Steel tube in produced is Three thickness classes --- Light, Medium and Heavy and available in black finish or hot dipped galvanized finish in 6.00 / 5.80 meter uniform mill lengths.

Application

For ordinary conveyance of steam, gas, air, water, etc.

End Finish and End Protection

Plain-end square-cut (PE) or threaded and fixed with coupling (T/C). (PE) tubes are shipped without any protection on both ends. T/C tubes are supplied screwed with taper threads to BS 21 and fitted with one taper-threaded malleable iron socket, as required under this specifications.
